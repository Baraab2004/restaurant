About Shreem Restaurant

Founded in 2018, Shreem Restaurant has grown into a popular destination for food enthusiasts and diners alike.

Our team of expert chefs and culinary experts has been working tirelessly to create a menu that caters to the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers.

Our commitment to quality and fresh ingredients ensures that our customers receive the best possible dining experience.

We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service, and we strive to make every meal a memorable experience.

Our restaurant is open from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday to Friday, and Sunday, saturday: Closed
